# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment
At **Hilal Technologic**, we are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free environment for everyone who participates in our projects and community.

We value professionalism, kindness, and constructive collaboration.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment include:
- Using respectful and inclusive language
- Being considerate of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and receiving constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the project

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech
- Personal attacks, insults, or trolling
- Publishing private or confidential information without permission
- Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

---

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies to all spaces managed by **Hilal Technologic**, including:
- GitHub repositories
- Issues and pull requests
- Discussions and community interactions

---

## Enforcement
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and taking appropriate action in response to unacceptable behavior.

If a violation occurs, maintainers may take actions including:
- Warning the offender
- Temporary or permanent removal from the project
- Restricting participation in community spaces

---

## Reporting
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it privately to the maintainers via GitHub.

All reports will be reviewed and handled fairly and confidentially.
